    • The Toyota Supra MK4, also known as the A80 Supra, is a legendary sports car known for its sleek design and exhilarating performance. Produced from 1993 to 2002, it features a powerful twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine, the famous 2JZ-GTE, delivering 320 horsepower. With its iconic looks, precise handling, and rear-wheel drive, the Supra MK4 offers a thrilling driving experience. Its popularity among car enthusiasts and appearances in popular culture, such as the "Fast and Furious" movies, have solidified its status as an iconic and highly sought-after classic sports car.

    • The BMW M5 is a high-performance luxury sedan that combines exceptional power, precise handling, and luxurious comfort. With its sleek and aggressive design, the M5 stands out on the road. Under the hood, it packs a potent engine, typically a twin-turbocharged V8, delivering blistering acceleration and impressive top speeds. The M5 is known for its exceptional driving dynamics, thanks to advanced technologies like an adaptive suspension, responsive steering, and an intelligent all-wheel drive system. Inside, the M5 offers a premium and driver-focused cabin with high-quality materials and advanced infotainment features. It represents the perfect fusion of luxury, performance, and everyday practicality.
